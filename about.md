origin_blocks:
  - body: "In 2018, Geoff Scott started something simple: a free, monthly morning gathering in NYC called Startup Lean Coffee. The idea was borrowed from the Lean Coffee™ format. A lightly facilitated, agenda-less meeting where participants show up, propose topics, vote, and talk."
    image: /assets/images/origin-2018-lean-coffee.jpg
    image_alt: "A 2018 Startup Lean Coffee session: wide view of the room with attendees seated and the Startup Lean Coffee README on the screen."
  - body: "No keynote speakers. No pitch decks. Just founders, investors, operators, advisors, and executives sitting around tables having genuine, raw conversations about the things that actually keep them up at night."
    image: /assets/images/origin-discussion.jpg
    image_alt: "Five attendees in candid discussion around a wood table covered in post-its."
  - body: "The conversations have spanned fundraising, product-market fit, go-to-market strategy, AI, co-founder conflict, hiring, pivots, and everything in between. More importantly, the relationships formed around those tables have led to real outcomes: members have become co-founders, grown their teams with key hires, found valuable resources, and built lasting friendships."
    image: /assets/images/origin-postits-writing.jpg
    image_alt: "Documentary detail of hands writing on a post-it surrounded by other topic notes on a wood table."
  - body: "In 2025, Benjamin Friedman and Karina Muller joined as co-organizers to grow and formalize our community, which now includes over 1,300 founders, advisors, and investors. It was clear that \"Startup Lean Coffee\" no longer captured what this had become. It wasn't just about startups. It wasn't just about coffee. It was about what happens when you pull up a chair, bring a real question, and trust the people around you to help you think it through."
    image: /assets/images/origin-coorganizers-2025.jpg
    image_alt: "Karina Muller and Benjamin Friedman in casual standing conversation."
  - body_lead: "So we gave it the name it deserved: <em>Table.</em>"
    body: "Today, Table runs regular gatherings in New York and beyond. In 2025 we held a session in Raleigh, North Carolina, and we're exploring other cities including the New York Capital Region. We're also adding new event formats alongside the original lean-coffee mornings."
